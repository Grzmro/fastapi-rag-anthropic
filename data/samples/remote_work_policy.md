# Acme Corp — Remote Work Policy (2024)

## Eligibility

All full-time employees who have completed their 90-day probation period are
eligible to work remotely. Contractors and interns are not covered by this
policy and must work on-site unless their manager files a written exception.

## Working hours

Remote employees are expected to be available during core hours, defined as
10:00 to 16:00 in their local time zone. Outside core hours, employees may
arrange their schedule freely as long as they complete 40 hours per week.
Time tracking is submitted every Friday by 18:00 through the internal portal.

## Equipment and expenses

The company provides a laptop and one external monitor to every remote
employee. Employees may claim up to 500 USD per year for home-office
equipment such as chairs, desks, or keyboards. Internet costs are reimbursed
at a flat rate of 40 USD per month. Reimbursement requests must include a
receipt and are processed within 30 days.

## Security requirements

Remote employees must connect to internal systems through the company VPN.
Passwords must be rotated every 90 days, and two-factor authentication is
mandatory for all accounts. Storing customer data on personal devices is
strictly prohibited.

## In-office days

Even fully remote employees are required to attend the office at least four
days per quarter, typically aligned with team planning sessions. Travel costs
for these mandatory visits are covered by the company for employees living
more than 100 kilometers from the office.
